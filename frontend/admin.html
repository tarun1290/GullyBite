⚠ WhatsApp Username Migration (June 2026)
Meta is rolling out WhatsApp usernames. Users who adopt a username may hide their phone number — webhooks will send a BSUID instead.
Action required: Enable the Meta Contact Book API for each WABA to request phone numbers from BSUID-only users. Go to Meta Business Suite → WhatsApp Manager → Settings → Contact Book and enable it for all active WABAs.
GullyBite already handles BSUID customers and collects phone numbers at payment time, but enabling Contact Book gives a fallback phone resolution path.
