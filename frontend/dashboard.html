How taxes work on GullyBite

GST (5%): Goods and Services Tax is collected from customers on food orders, packaging, and delivery charges. This is included in the prices shown to customers. You are responsible for filing GST returns with the collected amounts.

Platform Fee GST (18%): GullyBite charges GST on the platform commission. This is deducted from your settlements and GullyBite remits this to the government.

TDS (1%): Tax Deducted at Source is withheld on your net payouts as per Section 194-O of the Income Tax Act. TDS certificates are available for download from the TDS Summary tab above. You can claim credit for TDS when filing your income tax returns.
