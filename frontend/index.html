Complete order flow — from message to delivery

1

Customer messages "Hi"

Opens the 24-hour customer service window. Bot welcomes them and asks for their delivery location.

2

Location shared → nearest branch found

Haversine formula calculates distance to all branches. Nearest one within delivery radius is selected.

3

WhatsApp Catalog sent

That branch's live menu appears as an in-app shop. Customer browses, adds to cart inside WhatsApp.

4

Order placed → payment link sent

Customer confirms cart. Razorpay link generated and sent via WhatsApp. Expires in 15 minutes.

5

Payment confirmed → restaurant notified

Razorpay webhook fires. Order marked PAID. Restaurant dashboard shows new order. Customer gets confirmation.

6

Packed → dispatched → settled

Restaurant marks packed → 3PL dispatches → customer gets tracking updates → Monday settlement.
